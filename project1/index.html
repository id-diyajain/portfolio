Project Overview

A contemporary residential masterpiece celebrating warm earth tones, natural materials, and abundant natural light. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, the Amber Residence features open living spaces that flow seamlessly with intimate zones for relaxation. The luxurious master bedroom showcases sophisticated design with custom furnishings and elegant finishing touches.

Design Concept

The amber hues throughout the residence create a cohesive narrative that connects to the natural surroundings. Each room flows seamlessly, with carefully curated artwork and bespoke furniture pieces that enhance the overall aesthetic. The design prioritizes comfort without compromising elegance.

Materials & Finishes

Teak wood flooring, cream marble countertops, brass fixtures, linen upholstery, and handwoven textiles. The color palette features warm ambers, soft creams, and deep terracotta accents throughout.

Craftsmanship

Custom-designed headboard featuring geometric patterns with grooves for visual depth. Tailored storage solutions with wardrobe systems providing functionality and aesthetics. Premium lighting design with pendant fixtures and ambient cove lighting.
