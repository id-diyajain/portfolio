About the Designer

Designing with soul
& precision

Diya Jain is a celebrated interior designer whose work bridges the tactile warmth of organic materials with the discipline of architectural thinking. Based between Mumbai and Milan, her studio has shaped over 200 residences, hospitality projects, and cultural spaces across three continents.

Each commission begins not with mood boards but with listening — understanding how a client moves through space, what textures they seek, what silences they need. The result is interiors that feel inevitable.
